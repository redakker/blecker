BLEcker flash tool (beta)

You can upload the BLEcker binary to your board. Currently, full wipe is available only. (After the first flash use the OTA update to keep your settings.)

  • Connect your ESP32 board to your computer
  • Choose which version do you want to flash (latest is recommended)
  • Push the connect button and follow the further instructions
